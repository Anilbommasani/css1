pawan kalyan

Ram charan

Ram Charan is another prominent actor in the Telugu film industry, often referred to as Tollywood. He is the son of veteran actor Chiranjeevi and started his acting career in 2007 with the film "Chirutha." Since then, he has appeared in numerous successful Telugu films and has established himself as one of the leading actors in the industry.
